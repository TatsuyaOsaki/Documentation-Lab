Protocols#

Thawing cells from freezing cells

  1. Thaw AAV293 cells in 37°C waterbath.

  2. Add to 5 mL of 293T culture medium.

  3. Spin down 5 min at 1000 rpm

  4. Resuspend in 10 mL 293T culture medium.

  5. Plate in 75 cm 2 flask. No need for coating.

Important

Change medium every day or every two days. If you don’t and the medium gets yellow, the cells will detach and die.

  1. Once the cells are 90% confluent, split them 1 to 4 using trypsin:

  2. rince with PBS-

  3. 5 minutes in trypsin (7 to 8 mL per flask)

  4. Resuspend in 7 mL medium per flask.

  5. NB: For cells to be transfected: dissociate: add 7 mL medium to the flask and pipet up and down projecting the cells on the wall of the flask for 15 times.

  6. Spin down 5 min at 1000 rpm

  7. Resuspend in 8 mL medium

  8. Plate 2 mL cells + 8 mL medium per flask to split ¼.
